About Ad Post Graphics

A lifetime of marketing experience serving independent grocers and local businesses.

Our Story

Ad Post originated as an advertising agency in the fall of 1983. Over the years, the company expanded its services to support independent grocers, small chain grocery stores and local businesses with consulting, marketing, design, printing and media distribution.

Our long-standing client relationships are built on trust, consistency and professional service. We have worked with stores and markets across Pennsylvania, Ohio and New Jersey, helping them stay competitive through creative marketing and dependable production.
